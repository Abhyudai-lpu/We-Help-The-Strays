About Us

Nestled in the serene town of Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh, We Help The Strays (WHTS) is a compassionate initiative dedicated to transforming the lives of stray animals, especially dogs. Established in 2020 by siblings Sarthak and Surbhi Dogra, WHTS began as a heartfelt response to the pandemic’s devastating impact on stray animals. What started as an effort to feed and care for these voiceless beings during the crisis has blossomed into a full-fledged movement for animal welfare.


At the core of WHTS lies the unwavering belief in the philosophy of “Adopt, Don’t Shop.” This principle drives our mission to find loving homes for stray animals through regular adoption drives. Over the years, we have successfully connected countless stray dogs with families who share our vision of kindness and inclusion. By encouraging adoption over purchasing pets, we aim to challenge the culture of commercial breeding and give every stray a chance at a better life.


WHTS is not just about finding homes—it’s about creating a sustainable environment for strays. We organize regular vaccination campaigns, sterilization drives, and health checkups to ensure these animals receive the care they deserve. Our feeding programs, which started during the pandemic, continue to provide daily nourishment to strays across Dharamshala. These efforts are powered by a team of dedicated volunteers, veterinarians, and supporters who believe in our cause.


Sarthak and Surbhi Dogra, the sibling duo behind WHTS, have seamlessly combined their love for animals with their entrepreneurial spirit. Their café, SKOL Culture, located in the bustling town of McLeodganj, has become a hub for animal lovers and a platform to promote awareness about stray welfare. With every visit to SKOL Culture, patrons not only enjoy its vibrant ambiance but also contribute to the mission of WHTS, making it a unique blend of hospitality and philanthropy.


Beyond direct intervention, WHTS is committed to educating communities about humane treatment and coexistence with stray animals. Through workshops, events, and online campaigns, we aim to instill empathy and encourage individuals to take action in their neighborhoods. We believe that change begins with awareness, and every small step can lead to a significant impact.

Our journey at WHTS is fueled by hope, determination, and the belief that every stray animal deserves dignity, love, and a chance at life. As we continue to grow, our goal is to expand our reach, strengthen our initiatives, and inspire more people to join this movement of compassion.


We invite you to be a part of our story. Whether through adoption, volunteering, or donations, your support can help us rewrite the narrative for stray animals in Dharamshala and beyond. Together, we can create a future where no stray is left behind.

Let’s make a difference, one life at a time.
